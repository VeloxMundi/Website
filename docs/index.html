Velox Mundi Factori

A Quick World Maker

Important Note:
Velox Mundi is currently under development and is in an alpha-release stage. This means that the application is extremely unstable and not ready for the general public. Use this site and the Velox Mundi Desktop application at your own risk.

 

Velox Mundi Factori (Latin for "A Quick World Maker") is a world-building application for creating unlimited worlds with wiki-style pages. Worlds can be developed privately and published to any website when ready.
